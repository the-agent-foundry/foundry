# Agent Identity and Voice (Template)

This is who the agent is, not what it does. Operating instructions live in `AGENTS.template.md`. Identity and voice live here. Keeping them separate lets you change the job without changing the personality, and vice versa.

A consistent, deliberately shaped identity is one of the four things an agent has that a chatbot does not. It is why the output sounds like your standards instead of generic model defaults. Worth getting right.

Copy this file, fill it in, and load it as the agent's identity context.
